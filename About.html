About Us

We are the World's best innovation site

Our mission

Our long-term mission is to inspire young Sri Lankans to innovate for a better tomorrow To achieve this goal we've build the most comprehensive open source innovation knowledge base on the web. We publish tools, guides, news, inventors and inventions, courses to give knowledge to develop a successful new business. Also we are hoping connect inventors and consumers directly by building a essential online innovative market.

Innovate.lk

A s a developing country, recently Sri Lanka have taken various initiatives to improve innovations and related industry towards sustainable economy. As students of NSBM Green University we identified that the local market for new innovations has declined. As a solution we are tryting to introduce a platform to uplift it by publishing innovation. We expect local community, businessman to see the innovations and to help encourage. Innovate.lk will publish information and news about new inventions. So the business institutions searching for new inventions can connect the inventors through our platform. Therefore these new inventors wii get an opportunity to release the product to the market.
