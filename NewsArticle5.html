NERO – Sri Lanka’s next flagship PC game ‘Nero’ has been released.


  NERO created as a tribute to popular Sri Lankan Sniper Ranjith Madalana, who is named as one among the greatest 14 snipers in the world. The game was released by “Arimac”, Sri Lanka’s leading end-to-end Digital Solutions and Innovations organization Arimac Digital, Sri Lanka’s premier digital solutions company announces the launch of NERO – a fast-paced, stealth-action game developed 100% by Sri Lankan talent at Arimac Games game design studio.

Arimac Digital, Sri Lanka’s premier digital solutions company, recently unveiled the early access build of its upcoming stealth-action shooter and South Asia’s biggest game title – NERO. The game is now available for PC via the Steam video game digital distribution service.

NERO marks the commencement of Sri Lanka’s foray into the global stage of gaming showcasing the immense talent of Sri Lankan game developers. NERO is the love child of a group of highly driven game developers at Arimac, who spent countless hours conducting extensive research and developing a world-class gaming experience over three years to create a watershed game celebrating the heroism of unsung war heroes across South Asia. Over the past decennium, Arimac has molded over 30,000 young minds on digital experience and game development, through various community development programs, such as Arimac Hero. And with NERO, Arimac strive to continue their efforts on nurturing the minds of tomorrow by providing everyone in the Southeast Asian region a platform and the opportunity to make waves in the world of gaming.

Along with a PC title, NERO will include a mobile multiplayer title, a hyper-casual gaming title, a comic book series, a digital asset library, and branded merchandise.
