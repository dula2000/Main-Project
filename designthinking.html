Design Thinking Training

How to introduce
design thinking
into your company

Design thinking is a human-centered, repetitive process of understanding the user, challenging assumptions, and redefining problems. It consists of five basic stage: empathy, definition, idea, prototype and test.







Companies are increasingly recognizing the power of design thinking. But it naturally raises the question: How can you introduce this thinking into a new environment?



Here are a few ways to champion design thinking in your organization:




1. Start small

Design thinking is not something you can magically imprint throughout your company. It will take time to train others about the mindset first, then plan to implement each of the five stages.

So start small first. Build small experiments that allow your team to practice collecting data, checking frequently, and repeating quickly. By setting aside time for those skills you will set your mindset.




2. Identify previous adopters and evangelists

With the introduction of any new process or skill, there will always be people who naturally excel from the start. Some people have previous experience in design thinking or are particulary keen to take the time to learn about it.

Be sure to use these great people to create a network of supporters. These individuals can act as evangelizers for design thinking, leading the concept in meetings and project across departments, and also helping to train other employees along the way.




3. Avoid silos by department or team

Design thinking is multi-disciplinary and co-creative. It works best when it involves people who bring different perspectives and expertise to the table.

whether you are conducting a workshop to teach your organization about design thinking or starting a small project to gain practical experience, make sure your designteams include people across departments and disciplines.







4. Understand that design thinking is a fluid process

Creative thinking is not linear. The five stages should not follow a specific sequence and can occur in parallel. When introducing disign thinking at an early stage, do not try to apply the process from end to end, from empathy to examination to every problem.

Make sure you apply disign thinking in a way that is most meaningfull to your problem. For example, you can start testing and find that it creates new ideas for your project.




5. Identify how you will measure success

How do you measure and communicate th success of design thinking? When you spend a lot of time and effort introducing the process to your team, make sure you have a system in place to monitor progress. You can track the number of projects where design thinking is relevant. Or, conduct surveys to measure the impact of design thinking on employee satisfaction.







Boost innovation with design thinking
