Basic expectations from a business innovation model thinking

Analyzing business models

Understand the dynamics of business models and learn how to build different business models or ecosystems. Be inspired by other industries and explore which revenue models suit you best.

Gather effective tools for innovation

Participants will learn hands-on tools that help them generate new business models. By using tools to solve real business challenges, we make sure the mindset and methodologies get transferred to the ongoing business.

Industry specific content

These interactive online modules are designed for maximum engagement. You will practice the methodology with cases tailored to your organization.

Test new business model options

Hack your way through digital channels! Get an overview of which experiments exist and which are most applicable to your organization. Learn how to design digital experiments & build a validation plan.

Training Programs & Courses

We included this business model innovation training courses to master the skills needed by young innovators to grow a business related product. After analyzing few problems regarding innovations, we identified that over 70% of Sri Lankan innovations have not released to the local or global market as a finished sustainable product.

To overcome this our young innovators should develop their business skills. As an effort to this following links are provided to follow on business model training, which is compulsary for a innovator to build business model or grow a market for their products.

  • Strategyzer Academy
  • Business Model - Udemy
  • Business Model- EdX
  • Business Model Innovation - Board of innovations.
