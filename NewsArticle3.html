"Let's Get Rid of Covid 19" - Referral to Innovation Commercialization


  Channa Jayasumana, State Minister of Drug Production, Supply and Regulation, Minister of Skills Development, Vocational Education, Research and Innovation (Specialist Doctor) Sita Arambepola, Sri Lanka Commissioner of the Inventors' Commission Prof.. N. M. The. Mr. Sirimuthu, Assistant Commissioner Prof. Dr. Neelika Malavige, Assistant Commissioner Anupa Herath, Director / CEO, Inventors Commission of Sri Lanka N. W. Mr. Dolawatta, Ministry officials, innovators, media officers, investors, businessmen and many others participated.

A brief presentation by innovators on the need and commercial potential of their designs.

Microorganism Diverting and Clearing Device - Dr. Shashika Ariyaratne. Several advanced techniques have been used to disinfect patients' respiratory airways after they have been connected to artificial respiration machines. This innovation, which has been patented by Sri Lanka under the auspices of the Inventors' Commission of Sri Lanka and has been successfully tested, is currently being studied for further improvement in several government hospitals and universities.
Video Laryngoscope - Dr. Anupa Herath. A video laryngoscope is a medical device used by patients to fall asleep before surgery under general anesthesia, making it an essential and crucial step in administering artificial respiration to patients. Can. This tool is very helpful in difficult situations where traditional methods are challenged. This also reduces the need to be close. This is very important for the surgery of patients with or without COVID 19 infection.
Microorganism Destroying & Air Filtering Unit - Mr. Thushan Karunaratne A new air purifier that can remove and filter airborne particulate matter and microorganisms is a unique invention by Mr. Thushan Karunaratne. This new design, which has the unique features of purifying sterile air in a confined environment, controlling humidity, mixing fresh air safely, and signaling service requirements, is currently being installed and operational at the COVID Patient Treatment Center at Baddegama District Hospital. Designed to be used not only for ward complexes but also for domestic and public transport services, the new design has been upgraded with the assistance of the Inventors' Commission of Sri Lanka and has reached the commercial level after obtaining technical reports.
Smooth lateral patient transfer apparatus (Bed to Trolly) - Dr.P. M. T. Mr. Bandara.This device is specially designed to minimize injuries that may occur during the transfer of patients with spinal cord injuries. With this product, the number of health workers required for lateral transfer patients is reduced and only one health worker can easily transfer the patient. With this uniquely designed bed, even a patient who has undergone surgery can easily transfer from the operating room bed to the ward bed without changing the position of the patient. As the transfer process is seamless, this product may be particularly useful in dealing with COVID-19 patients in intensive care units.
