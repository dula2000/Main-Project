Ino8 Robotics Pvt

Under the guidance of the Inventors Commission of Sri Lanka, Ino8 Robotics Pvt. The Department of Wildlife Conservation has agreed to purchase 50 locally made collars for wild elephants for the year 2021, an invention that seeks to secure their intellectual property rights. The project, which is valued at over Rs. 22 million, is expected to save a large amount of money flowing abroad, and the technology, which is more advanced than the collars currently in use, will be available at a lower cost.
